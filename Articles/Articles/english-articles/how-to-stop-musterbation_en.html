A Proven Method to Quit Masturbation in Just 7 Days

✍️ By Ahmed Trochman | 🗓️ 16 April 2026

Introduction

Masturbation has become one of the most common and widespread issues today. The reason is simple: a lack of full awareness about using technology in a correct and safe way, which may lead to exposure to pornographic content and then to masturbation. After finishing this article, you will be able to stop masturbation completely and it will no longer be a problem for you again. Let’s begin

Main Topics

  1. Strong Psychological Preparation (You Are in Control)
  2. The Incredible Benefits of Lowering Your Gaze
  3. Healthy Thinking and Cleaning the Mind from Porn
  4. Loneliness as a Major Cause of Porn Addiction
  5. Kegel Exercises
  6. Following a Proper Diet
  7. Avoid Looking at Genital Areas
  8. Controlled Masturbation in Case You Cannot Stop
  9. Benefits of Quitting Masturbation

1- Strong Psychological Preparation (You Are in Control)

The first and most important step is to understand that you are in control, not a person trapped in a cycle of pornography and masturbation. You must completely change this mindset because the truth is that you can exit this cycle at any time you choose, easily.

The key idea here is to reprogram your thinking so that you see yourself as the one in control. When you truly believe this, it builds self-confidence and makes you stronger mentally and more capable of stopping anytime without feeling helpless.

2- The Incredible Benefits of Lowering Your Gaze

Before understanding why lowering the gaze is one of the strongest ways to avoid pornography and masturbation, we must understand that the brain is highly affected by what we see.

Humans rely heavily on visual stimulation; anything you see affects your emotions and behavior.
Example: A beautiful landscape makes you calm and relaxed, while a fight scene makes you stressed and upset.

In the same way, looking at the opposite gender in a sexualized way creates stimulation in the brain that can turn into desire.
This is where lowering the gaze comes in—it prevents this stimulation from happening in the first place.

With consistent practice of lowering the gaze in daily life (street, phone, TV), the brain gradually reduces its response to such triggers, and desire becomes calmer and more natural.

In the end, this helps reduce pornography and masturbation gradually and effectively 👍.

3- Healthy Thinking and Cleaning the Mind from Porn

Your way of thinking determines your entire life. A successful and unsuccessful person may have the same abilities, but the real difference lies in mindset. When you change your thinking for the better, your life changes for the better, and optimism and trust in God play a major role in that.

The Prophet ﷺ said: “Be optimistic about good, and you will find it.” This highlights the importance of positive thinking. And in a divine hadith, Allah says: “I am as My servant thinks of Me,” meaning your expectations and thoughts have a major impact on your life results.

To stop pornography and masturbation, you must first believe that you can control yourself and are not a slave to the habit. Repeating this belief strengthens your willpower and confidence.

By avoiding sexual thoughts, content, and fantasies + lowering the gaze, triggers gradually decrease, desire weakens, and you gain stronger control over yourself and your life.

4- Loneliness as a Major Cause of Porn Addiction

One of the most important things to understand is that the mind becomes weaker against porn thoughts when a person is alone, because isolation gives more space for fantasies. That’s why it is important to break isolation and always stay around people or in an environment with movement and sound, even without direct interaction.

Another powerful method is to stay busy, because the brain cannot focus on two things at once. If sexual thoughts appear, immediately change your state: study, move, sit with family, or do any simple activity. After a few minutes, the thoughts will fade.

As the saying goes: “If you do not occupy yourself with truth, falsehood will occupy you.” Free time is a major cause of relapse, while being busy restores focus and balance.

5- Kegel Exercises

Here we are talking about the physical impact of pornography and masturbation, and how Kegel exercises can help reduce sexual stimulation. Kegel exercises target pelvic muscles and improve control and physical awareness.

Important note:
You can search for how to properly do Kegel exercises on YouTube or online to learn detailed instructions.

In simple terms, excessive masturbation may lead to increased blood flow to sexual areas, while Kegel exercises help improve circulation and strengthen control muscles.

It is recommended to practice Kegel exercises daily (for example twice a day at first), then gradually reduce depending on comfort. It is a helpful habit for recovery.

6- Following a Proper Diet

Diet has a major impact on both physical and mental health, and therefore on hormones and sexual drive. Eating healthy foods such as vegetables, drinking enough water, eggs, and meat helps maintain balance.

At the same time, you should reduce processed foods like chips, snacks, and soft drinks, as they may affect mood and energy levels.

Some suggest that garlic in moderation may help reduce excessive stimulation, along with foods that support digestion such as apples and olive oil.

Bread should be consumed in moderation (2–3 pieces per day depending on needs). The main idea is maintaining a balanced diet to support both body and mind stability.

7- Avoid Looking at Genital Areas

One helpful method to reduce masturbation is avoiding looking at genital areas, especially during recovery. The brain links visual attention to increased stimulation, so avoiding this breaks that connection over time.

After using the bathroom, it is better to finish quickly and avoid unnecessary focus on these areas.

The bigger issue is usually pornography itself, not masturbation alone, because porn creates addiction, mental distraction, and mood swings. Reducing it makes control much easier.

8- Controlled Masturbation in Case You Cannot Stop

This method suggests masturbating only when sexual desire becomes very high, without watching pornography or using sexual fantasies.

The idea is to separate the act from external stimulation, so your brain becomes less dependent on visual or imaginary triggers.

Over time, the brain may stop reacting strongly to external triggers. However, if you can stop completely, that is always the best option.

9- Benefits of Quitting Masturbation

Quitting masturbation brings many psychological and physical benefits. Over time, you will notice improved mood, higher confidence, more energy, and better focus.

1- A more natural life and mental peace without constant porn thoughts. 2- Improved mood, confidence, and self-esteem. 3- Increased energy and vitality. 4- Better focus and productivity. 5- Inner peace and spiritual improvement. 6- Building a better life and achieving goals step by step.

Full Summary of Recovery Journey

In the end, recovering from masturbation depends mainly on changing your entire lifestyle, not just making a temporary decision. The real beginning is changing your mindset and believing you can control yourself completely.

Over time, reducing triggers like pornography, avoiding isolation and free time helps significantly in reducing urges and restoring self-control.

A healthy diet, structured daily routine, staying busy with goals, and supportive habits like Kegel exercises all strengthen willpower and stability.

With consistency, you will notice gradual improvements in mood, energy, and confidence, leading to a more balanced life focused on real goals.
